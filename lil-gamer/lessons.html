Lessons Learned

Throughout this project I learned a number of surprising things. I learned how to calculate power consumption and estimate battery life (in the completion of the analysis page). I learned how to work with Fusion 360 and custom parts in order to create my schematic (also in the analysis page). I learned that common retail electrical products may contain some complex technology in order to accomplish a simple task - I definitely did not expect an RLC circuit to connect an audio signal to a speaker. I also learned that 0 ohm resistors exist, presumably as a larger and more reliable "bridge" connection!
