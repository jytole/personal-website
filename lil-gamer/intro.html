About

I am Kyler Smith, an undergraduate in Computer Engineering at Mississippi State University with an interest in psychology! I work for the Social, Therapeutic, and Robotic Systems Lab. This website was created for my ECE 4724: Embedded Systems Class.

This website was created to showcase the disassembly and analysis of the Fisher-price: Laugh & Learn: Lil' Gamer. I chose this product because I am interested in how features of a handheld video game were simulated on a scale suitable for a Fisher-price children's toy.

A specific feature I am interested in investigating is the ability of it to hold a state in order to process sequences of key presses as long as the Konami Code (Up, Up, Down, Down, Left, Right, Left, Right, B, A). The process and results are detailed on the subpages of this website.
